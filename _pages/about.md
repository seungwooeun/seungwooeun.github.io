I am **Seungwoo Eun** (은승우), a researcher in **Human–Computer Interaction (HCI)**, **Virtual and Augmented Reality (VR/AR)**, and **3D User Interfaces**. My research focuses on designing and evaluating interaction techniques for immersive environments—such as portal-based remote object manipulation and locomotion in virtual reality—to make spatial interaction more efficient, precise, and comfortable.

I recently completed my **M.S. in Artificial Intelligence** at **Pusan National University**, where I worked in the **XR Lab** under the supervision of Prof. Myungho Lee. Before that, I received my **B.S. in Electrical and Computer Engineering** from Pusan National University in 2024.

Please feel free to reach out via [email](mailto:aglraswe@gmail.com)!

Research Interests

* 3D User Interfaces and interaction techniques
